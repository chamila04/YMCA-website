About Us

Welcome to the Kandy Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA)! We're a community-centered organization committed to making a positive impact right here in Kandy. At YMCA, we believe in the power of community engagement and collaboration to address the important social issues facing our local area.

Our roots trace back to a strong tradition of service and compassion, inspired by Christian values. However, our doors are open to everyone, regardless of background or belief. We embrace diversity and inclusivity, striving to create a welcoming environment where all are valued and respected.

One of the ways we serve our community is by providing affordable and accessible accommodation options. Whether you're visiting Kandy for business or pleasure, our well-appointed rooms offer a comfortable and convenient place to stay. In addition to accommodation, we also offer recreational facilities for our members and guests to enjoy. Our badminton court is available for booking, providing an opportunity for friendly competition and exercise. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking to have some fun, our court is the perfect place to stay active and socialize.

But YMCA is more than just a place to stay or play sports. We're a hub for community engagement and empowerment. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to address the root causes of social issues, empowering individuals and families to lead healthier, happier lives. From youth development programs to adult education and job training, we're dedicated to making a difference, one person at a time.

Join us at the Kandy YMCA and be a part of something bigger than yourself. Together, we can build a stronger, more vibrant community for all.
